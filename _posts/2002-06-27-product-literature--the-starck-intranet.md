HESS Intranet Site Development  
Development and implementation of a web based HESS intranet site for use within H.C. Starck worldwide.

Including:  
· Requirement identification.  
· Functionality and site design.  
· Graphical User Interface design.  
· Content development.  
o Health and Safety training modules  
o Health and Safety information (Contractor Qualification Program, New Employee Orientation materials)  
o Regulatory updates  
o Dynamic information exchange / Q&A capability  
· Site management.
